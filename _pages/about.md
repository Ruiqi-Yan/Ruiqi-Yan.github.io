# About Me
Hi! My name is Ruiqi Yan (延瑞琦), an undergraduate student at Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU), majoring in Computer Science.

My research interests focus on Multimodal LLM, Audio and Speech, Human-Computer Interaction, and other related areas.


## Education
Undergraduate @ [Shanghai Jiao Tong University](https://www.sjtu.edu.cn/)  
School of Computer Science, Zhiyuan Honors Program  
Major: Computer Science and Technology  
<sub>*2023.9-present | Expected to graduate in 2027.6*</sub>
